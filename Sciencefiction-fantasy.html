Science Fiction & Fantasy

Science fiction, or sci-fi, is a genre of speculative fiction that explores imaginative concepts related to advanced scientific and technological innovations, space exploration, time travel, and extraterrestrial life.


The fantasy genre is a type of fiction characterized by magical, supernatural, or otherwise unreal elements, such as magic, mythical creatures, or imaginary worlds, which are central to the story's plot, setting, or characters.

  • Back to the future (1985)

    • teenager Marty McFly is accidentally sent back to 1955 in a time-traveling DeLorean created by his eccentric scientist friend, Doc Brown. To ensure his own future, he must prevent his future parents from falling in love and find a way to return to his own time.


  • 2001: A Space Odyssey (1968)

    • a mysterious black monolith's influence on human evolution from prehistoric times to space exploration. When an alien monolith is discovered on the Moon, a mission to Jupiter is launched on the spaceship Discovery, manned by astronauts and the sentient supercomputer HAL 9000.


  • Original Star Wars Trilogy ( 1977-1983)

    • young farmhand Luke Skywalker joins the Rebel Alliance to fight the oppressive Galactic Empire. With help from friends like Princess Leia, Han Solo, and droids C-3PO and R2-D2, he learns the ways of the mystical Force from mentors Obi-Wan Kenobi and Yoda, ultimately facing the evil Sith Lord Darth Vader and the Emperor in a quest for redemption and hope for the galaxy.


  • The Matrix (1999)

    • Set in the 22nd century, The Matrix tells the story of a computer hacker who joins a group of underground insurgents fighting the vast and powerful computers who now rule the earth.


  • The Lord of The Rings Trilogy (2001-2003)

    • Set in the fictional world of Middle-earth, the films follow the hobbit Frodo Baggins as he and the Company of the Ring embark on a quest to destroy the One Ring to defeat its maker, the Dark Lord Sauron.


  • Interstellar (2014)

    • The adventures of a group of explorers who make use of a newly discovered wormhole to surpass the limitations on human space travel and conquer the vast distances involved in an interstellar voyage.


  • Jurassic Park (1993)

    • A wealthy entrepreneur secretly creates a theme park featuring living dinosaurs drawn from prehistoric DNA. Before opening day, he invites a team of experts and his two eager grandchildren to experience the park and help calm anxious investors. However, the park is anything but amusing as the security systems go off-line and the dinosaurs escape.
